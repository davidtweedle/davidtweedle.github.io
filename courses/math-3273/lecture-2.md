### Definitions in this video ###
*Rowspace* - span of the rows of the matrix

*Column-space* - span of the columns of the matrix

*Null-space* - set $$ \{ \mathbf{x}\in F^n \ \mid \ A\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{0}\} $$

*Left null-space* - $$ \mathrm{null}(A^T) $$
